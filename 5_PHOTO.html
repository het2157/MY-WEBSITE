Campaign Rules

We try to keep the campaign clean and simple to participate. There are only a few rules for photos to participate in the contest in India. Every submission should be:

  1. All entries must be your own work; do not manipulate original images or use visible watermarks.
  2. All photos must be uploaded within July 2025 to be eligible.
  3. All photos must be licensed under the CC BY-SA license.
  4. Participants must have an active email account for contact.
  5. Upload photos of Natural Monuments in India only.
  6. Captions and descriptions are required.
